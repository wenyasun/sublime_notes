Article Writing Standard

Literature Review

  • Problem 1: how to introduce a paper properly?
    1. Use Surname of the first author, for example Sun et al. [1] proposed ...
    2. For number of authors less than 3, use Sun, Chen and Huang [2]
    3. Surname usually appears in the last
    4. Abbreviation usually appears in the firstname and middlename, **.
    5. For names: Fabrizio Cerreto,Bo Friis Nielsen,Otto Anker Nielsen,and Steven S. Harrod.
      Surnames are: Cerreto, Nielsen, Nielsen, Harrod respectively.
      XX (methods) presented by Sun et al. [1]
  • Use two research areas or two areas of focus -- other than two researches
  • How to refer a paper in later part of the paragraph?
    Use "This study... / The authors.../Among the interesting findings,../ The authors suggest that..." /Sun and Chen find/ In this case,/ this model/ the downside(upside) / been adopted by Sun et al./Sun et al. compare / in accordance with Sun and Chen
    In a comparison with, the study finds -- in accordance with Sun and Chen [1]
    This method describes..
    Sun and Chen extend...
    Based on..., the authors identify/use/combine/studies/apply/explores/preform/ provides/assess
    The .. presented above
    This research lacks a standardized methodology
  • Preceding train / Succeeding train
  • Logical words: Moreover,similarly, however, Further, is yet to be explored, the result shows that, Tables 1 and 2 summarize the literature just reviewed. In comparison to..., more rubust against, indicates, deploy, grow exponentially
  • use present tense when introduce a paper, introduce, identify,
